--- title: 'Bridge Projects' permalink: /projects/ ---

Carbon fiber laminate damage segmentation

Image segmentation provides an automated solution to Necstlab's manual labeling of damage in carbon fiber laminates.

Athlete identification from pose

Skeletal configurations extracted by CMU's OpenPose can be used to identify players from video.

Screening for locations in the genome that are safe to engineer

In a joint project with MIT's ProbComp group, probabilistic computing assists the Voigt Lab in finding optimal genome locations for gene insertion.

Flow control for low-cost solar-powered desalination

A Reinforcement Learning-based controller helps the Global Engineering and Research Lab optimize a desalination system that will provide clean drinking water to rural India.
